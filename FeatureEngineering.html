RPE











RPE, the rate of perceived exertion, is a numeric estimate of intensity during workouts. It ranges from 6-20, where 6 is the lowest intensity and 20 is the highest. RPE is known to be correlated with heart rate; more specifically, $RPE=heart\_rate/10$.
